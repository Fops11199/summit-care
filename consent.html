Consent to Treat

By using Summit Care, you consent to receive medical care and treatment from our providers as described below.

  • Medical Services
  • Telemedicine
  • Risks & Benefits
  • Patient Rights
  • Withdraw Consent
  • Contact

Medical Services

You authorize Summit Care and its providers to deliver healthcare services as needed, including diagnosis, treatment, and follow-up care. All care is provided by licensed professionals in accordance with best practices and legal requirements.

Telemedicine

You consent to receive care via telemedicine when appropriate. Telemedicine involves the use of electronic communications to enable healthcare providers to evaluate, diagnose, and treat patients remotely.

  • Video consultations
  • Remote monitoring
  • Secure messaging
Risks & Benefits
  • Benefits: Convenience, access to specialists, timely care
  • Risks: Technical failures, privacy concerns, limitations of remote assessment

Patient Rights

  • Right to ask questions and receive clear answers
  • Right to refuse or withdraw consent at any time
  • Right to privacy and confidentiality
  • Right to access your medical records
Withdraw Consent

You may withdraw your consent for treatment at any time by notifying your provider or contacting Summit Care. Withdrawal of consent may affect your ability to receive certain services.

Contact

If you have questions about this consent, please contact us.
