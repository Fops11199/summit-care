Introduction

Summit Care complies with all applicable healthcare data protection laws, including HIPAA (for the U.S.), GDPR (for EU residents), and local data governance regulations. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your information is handled with the highest standards of security and transparency.

Information We Collect
  • Personal details (name, contact, date of birth)
  • Medical history & patient records
  • Insurance and billing information
  • Technical data (IP address, device type, browser)
How We Use Your Information
  • Delivering healthcare services
  • Appointment scheduling & reminders
  • Billing & insurance processing
  • Improving patient care through analytics
  • Communicating important updates
Data Sharing & Disclosure
  • Shared only with authorized healthcare providers
  • Insurance partners (with your consent)
  • Legal obligations (e.g., court orders, regulatory requests)
  • Never sold to third parties
